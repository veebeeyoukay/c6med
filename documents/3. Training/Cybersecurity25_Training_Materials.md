## Slide 9: Topic 5 - Phishing Response
**When You Spot a Phish**

**DO:**
1. **Stop** - Don't click anything
2. **Screenshot** - Capture evidence
3. **Report** - Forward to IT/Management
4. **Delete** - Remove from inbox
5. **Alert** - Warn colleagues if targeted

**DON'T:**
- Click links or attachments
- Reply or engage
- Forward to others (except IT)
- Feel embarrassed - it happens!

**If You Clicked:** Report immediately - speed matters more than pride
